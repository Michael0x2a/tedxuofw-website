our [volunteers][vol] page for full details and our [contact][con] page

## Technologies used

Our code is hosted on [Github][git] -- check it out!

We used the following technologies, frameworks, fonts, and assets:

-   Frameworks
    -   [Twitter Bootstrap](http://getbootstrap.com/) with [Glyphicons](http://glyphicons.com(
    -   [Jekyll](http://jekyllrb.com/)
    -   [Sass](http://sass-lang.com/)
-   Fonts
    -   Wisdom Script, a font from [Lost Type](http://www.losttype.com/wisdom_script/)
    -   [Font Awesome](http://fortawesome.github.io/Font-Awesome/)
-   Scripts
    -   [Hyphenator.js](http://code.google.com/p/hyphenator/)
    -   [jQuery](http://jquery.com/) and [jQuery UI](https://jqueryui.com/)
    -   [Modernizr](http://modernizr.com/)
    -   [FancyBox 2](fancyapps.com/fancybox/)
-   Images and media assets
    -   UW Student Life
-   Misc
    -   Hosting from [Bluehost](http://www.bluehost.com/)
    -   Domain registration from [Godaddy](http://www.godaddy.com)
    -   Analytics from [Google Analytics](http://www.google.com/analytics/)
    -   Contact form processing via [Simpleform](http://getsimpleform.com)
    -   Registration form via [Google Forms](http://drive.google.com)
